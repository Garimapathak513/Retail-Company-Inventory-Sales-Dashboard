# Retail Inventory & Sales Dashboard

Power BI dashboard analyzing 27K in sales, 39% profit margin, and inventory health.

## Key Insights
- Groceries = 33% of sales (top category)
- 18–25 age group = 42.5% of revenue
- Women = 62% of total sales
- February = peak sales month
- Premium Effect 266 = slow mover (68 days)
- 6 products on low stock alert
